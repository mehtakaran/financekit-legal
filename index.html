Frequently Asked Questions

Are the calculations accurate?

FinanceKit uses standard Australian lending formulas for all calculations. Results are estimates based on the inputs you provide and assume a fixed interest rate over the loan term. Always verify figures with your lender or a licensed financial adviser.

What does fortnightly repayment mean?

FinanceKit uses true accelerated fortnightly repayments, which means you pay half the monthly repayment every two weeks. This results in 26 payments per year (equivalent to 13 monthly payments) which reduces your loan faster than standard monthly repayments.

How does the extra repayment calculator work?

Enter an extra amount in the repayment calculator and FinanceKit will show you how many years and months you save off your loan and the total interest saved over the life of the loan.

How do I export a PDF?

Tap the download icon at the top right of any calculator screen to generate and share a PDF summary of your results.

Does FinanceKit store my data?

Your inputs are saved locally on your device only so you can return to your last calculation. No data is sent to any server or shared with third parties.

Is this financial advice?

No. FinanceKit is a calculator tool and provides estimates for informational purposes only. It does not constitute financial advice. Please consult a licensed financial adviser or mortgage broker before making financial decisions.

About FinanceKit

FinanceKit is an Australian home loan calculator app built to help homeowners understand their mortgage. It includes a repayment calculator, refinance comparison tool and PDF export.
