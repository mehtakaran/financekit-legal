Privacy Policy

FinanceKit

Last updated: March 2026

FinanceKit does not collect, store or share any personal information. All data you enter stays on your device.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how FinanceKit ("we", "our" or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

FinanceKit is a calculator app. It does not require an account, does not connect to any server, and does not transmit any data off your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. FinanceKit does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying details.

The only data the app stores is your last calculator inputs, saved locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device.

Device Permissions

FinanceKit does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos or any other device feature.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any other analytics or tracking service. We do not track your behaviour within the app.

Advertising

The current version of FinanceKit does not display advertisements. Future versions may include ads served by Google AdMob. If ads are introduced, this policy will be updated to reflect that and Google's privacy policy will apply to any data processed for ad delivery.

Third Party Services

FinanceKit does not integrate with any third party services, social networks, or external APIs. No data is shared with any third party.

Children

FinanceKit is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

PDF Export

When you export a PDF, it is generated entirely on your device and shared only through your own chosen method (such as email or AirDrop). We do not receive a copy of any exported PDF.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@financekit.app.
